About Us Page


TNK Technologies Ltd is a technology company specialising in the design, additive manufacturing and distribution of its 3d printed products via their subsidiary, 3dworks.

Thus we operate on a just in time basis pending consumer requirements.Our firm’s ethos is manufacturing sustainable plastics using biodegradable polyactic acid (PLA) raw materials.

PLA is a thermoplastic monomer derived from renewable, organic sources such as corn starch or sugar cane. Our sustainability credentials have been certified by the Environmental Agency, pursuant from the Environmental Product Declarations, EPDs.
