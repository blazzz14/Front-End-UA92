Privacy Policy


Your personal data is handled pursuant to the Data Protection Act 2018 and the GDPR regulations. This covers the following aspects:
  1. How the client interacts with the Web Application
  2. Cookies and Third Party Services
  3. How the client contacts us
  4. The Web Store
  5. Data Storage

1. How the client interacts with the Web Application

When the client visits our Web Application, the following information is processed:

  • User location
  • Type of browser
  • IP address
  • Personal data entered in our forms


2. Cookies and Third Party Services

Client data will also be processed via "cookies" or other third party services. Cookies are text files that are stored on your device which compile a user's settings, browsing history and preferences. The 3d Works server will have access to these small text files on your device.
By using our platform you consent your data being stored and processed in the above manner by our servers and 3rd party partners.

3. How the client contacts us

When a client subscribes to our newsletter the following information will be processed:

  • Personal data
  • Email
The client provides this data voluntarily; should they wish to revoke this consent, an "unsubscribe" link is provided on the newsletters or alternatively on 3dworks.co.uk.


4. The Web Store

Prior to the client utilising our Web Store, they are required to sign up for a 3d Works customer account. The customer account comprises of a Unique Customer Reference number. The following personal data is required for an account:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Billing address and Shipping address details
  • Contact phone number
  • Credit or Debit card details
  • Correspondence data

The customer account hosts previously processed data such as wish lists and favourites; Product reviews and previous help/assistance requests with the customer service team.


5. Data Storage

Data is stored for the intended purposes for as long as it is reasonably necessary. The storage of a user's personal details is stored for the duration of the account being active; upon termination personal details are stored in accordance with the Data Protection Act and the GDPR regulations.
