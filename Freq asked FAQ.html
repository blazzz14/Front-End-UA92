Frequently Asked Questions.
What is 3d printing?

3d printing is the addtitive manufacturing process that utilises 3d printing machines (ie 3d printers) to manufacture products layer by layer using recyclable raw materials such as Polyactic Acid (PLA).


Placing an order

Placing an order is straightforward! You may use the search bar or alternatively, the navigation pane which will assist in locating the desired products.
If the product is out of stock or unavailable- please kindly get in touch. A quote will be provided based on your specific requirements for the manufacturing process. Get in touch here


Delivery costs

Delivery costs are free for orders above £50. Otherwise, a £4.99 standard delivery charge applies. Lead delivery times are between 3-5 working days.
